Create a 5+ minute AI-generated video from plain English. The app creates scene plans, generates AI images, narrates with browser speech, and records a downloadable WebM file.

How this works

  1. Turns your plain-English command into a multi-scene structure.
  2. Uses AI image generation (Pollinations) per scene.
  3. Adds automated narration using browser text-to-speech.
  4. Records a continuous video in-browser and lets you download it.
